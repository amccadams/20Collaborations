Artist Bio

Communicating in unfamiliar territory, much like traveling to a new country, proved to be intimidating and exhilarating. To become a student again, refreshed the creative process, and reminded me to be compassionate and patient as I assist others in their own discovery. My book is very special to me as it reminds me of the cyclical and shifting process of grief, a necessary path towards renewal.

b. 1975
Nashville, TN
Melba Williams-Kirk is an award-winning filmmaker and educator from Nashville, TN. Her short films have screened in various venues, including PBS-POV and Sundance.
