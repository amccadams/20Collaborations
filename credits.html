Special Thanks from Britt

To all participants for their beautiful work, willingness to agree to be a participant in my crazy project, and the trust they placed in me.

To the Nashville Public Library for affording me a venue to showcase the results of the year long project without knowing what the results would be!

To the gallery director, Liz Coleman for trusting whole heartedly that the resulting work would be amazing.

To Amanda McCadams and Digital Wonder Cabinet, a partner from day one. This exhibit could not be complete without her outstanding design and production of the 20 Collaborations in Book Art website. Amanda has worked with me for over a year on this project on a strictly volunteer basis. In other words, she donated her time and talents for free. There are no words to accurately reflect my gratitude…….

To Mark Mosrie who volunteered his time and talents as the photographer of works produced. Mark opened his studio to us and labored over all works produced to document the full experience each book affords.

To Scott Thom for his support and project assistance throughout the year. I could not have pulled this off without him.

Thank you to Leslie Haines, Susan G. Hulme, and Alan LeQuire for lending their works to the exhibit.

Project Funding

No money was exchanged for 20 Collaborations in Book Art. Other than the artists being responsible for their own materials, the project was not funded or sponsored by any outside parties. This collaborative project relied on all participants to lend their talents and skills purely for the love of making art and the project itself.
