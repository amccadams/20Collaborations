Artist Bio

When asked why he agreed to be a part of 20 Collaborations in Book Art, he replied, “ This project afforded me the opportunity to truly collaborate with Britt beyond dioramas’ we made together when she was a child. It was wonderful to see how both our talents could come together in the form of an artist book.”

Robert L. Stadig lives in Princeton, Maine. He retired from a 40-year career with AT&T over 6 years ago and is a proud Navy veteran. Robert devotes his time to wood working, fishing, is a member of the American Legion, and volunteers for the state of Maine maintaining trails with the Tomah Country Trail Riders ATV Club. Robert began woodworking when he bought an old New England farmhouse in Connecticut with his wife Bonnie that was in desperate need of restoration. Not only did he do most of the carpentry himself, he also began making Shaker reproduction pieces to furnish the home. Those projects that were born out of necessity began a life long love for building, designing, and creating with wood. “I love the feel, look, and texture of different types of wood. I can spend hours picking out materials for the next project at the lumberyard.”
