About the Collaboration
Artist Bio

The timing of Britt’s invitation to collaborate on this project was fortuitous. Not only would retirement afford me more time to devote to studio practice than I’d been able to manage in many years, but the novelty of the challenge seemed a good fit at this juncture.
That said, the process has been frustrating, challenging, and ultimately satisfying. I am decidedly not a 3-dimensional artist, yet the book evolved into a very 3-dimensional form. I had never collaborated, but found the process both positive and intriguing. Britt has been exceptionally helpful, providing useful input and, of course, providing the book ‘cover’ that I could not. I think few people possess Britt’s subtlety or gracious spirit. It is her part that has made this such a pleasurable experience.

b. 1947
Louisville, KY
Claire Hampton is originally from Louisville, KY, and earned a B.F.A. and an M.A. from Murray State University in Kentucky. She lives in Hendersonville, TN, and recently retired from teaching at Volunteer State Community College.
