About the Collaboration
Artist Bio

This piece is an illustrated memorial of 3 men who made the ultimate sacrifice while serving in combat. All of them were members of the 10th Mountain Division, Fort Drum, NY. I was fortunate enough to know each one of them and blessed to call them my brothers. There's a saying that usually accompanies the fallen soldier memorial: "All give some. Some give all." For these 3 young men, all is exactly what they gave.

b. 1983
West Palm Beach, FL
Currently living in East Nashville. Spent 5 years on Active Duty in the U.S. Army. Primary job was Infantry. Did 2 Combat Tours: Afghanistan from Aug '03-May '04, and Iraq from Aug '05-Aug '06. Started college after the Army in 2007. Graduated from The Art Institute of TN-Nashville in Dec '09 with a BFA in Graphic Design. Currently a full-time freelance designer with a primary specialty in Healthcare related design and marketing.
