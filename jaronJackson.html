About the Collaboration
Artist Bio

I've really enjoyed being a part of this project. I had been looking for a project to get me off the computer and to use my hands again. I feel so honored to have been invited by Britt to be a part of a show and to be among so many great artists. While working on this project, I've completed two other paintings, worked on carving a new linoleum block and started a new art piece.
In a world where our lives are being consumed by digital media, it's great to have a show that exhibits the beauty of book arts.

I've been working as a graphic designer for over ten years. In my experience I've worked on a variety of projects spanning both print and web formats that have helped me develop strong analytical and creative problem solving skills. Currently I'm an Art Director and UX/UI team lead for redpepper in Nashville, TN.
